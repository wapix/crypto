RARITY

Our series present variations of elements, such as background, accessories, etc. that differentiate one non-fungible token from another of the same series. These variations are called traits.

Rarity is one of the most key factors in determining the value of an individual non-fungible token. If you want to sell your NFT, you must check the value of such NFT. You can easily determine that an NFT has some rare traits by looking at its properties.
