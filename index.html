FAQ

What is PYOBeer?
PYOBeer is the first manufacturer and distributor of craft beer in the metaverse.
We will create our craft beer and produce the NFT with the craft beer brand as a
promotion. Our community can invest, run, and taste craft beer together.
Why should I invest in PYOBeer?
Each crypto beer NFT is an access token that gives one the privilege to be a Brewer
of our community.
How do you buy a craft beer NFT?
We will announce the pre sales mint date for Whitelist member and mint date for
member. Pls ready the Metamask Wallet with ETH to mint. We’ll use polygon with
ERC1155 to decrease the GAS FEE.
How can I sell my PYOBeer?
If you are starting, you can also try seeking NFT collectors online. An excellent place
to start would be Opensea, where many NFT collectors lurk, and they might turn out
to be your next buyer.
Do holders of PYOBeer stand a chance to gain anything?
10% of royalty will be a giveaway pool to all Brewers. There will be a lucky draw or mini
-game biweekly for all members to join and get the ETH until the next round of NFT release.
How do you know your NFT is authentic?
NFT ownership is recorded on the blockchain, and that entry acts as a digital pink slip.
